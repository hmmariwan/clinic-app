DR DAVID TERRY
PRINCIPAL DENTIST

David Terry qualified with Phd from UCL London in 1990 and was awarded the University of London Gold Medal as the most distinguished student. He was also awarded the Certificate of Honour, the Ronald Gain Prosthetics Certificate of Merit, the Saunders Prize, and the S. J. Kaye Prize. He became a member of the Royal College of Surgeons of England and followed this up with training in the provision of veneers and cosmetic crowns. He then completed postgraduate training in Restorative Dentistry and Implant Dentistry, both of which he passed with distinction from the Eastman Dental Institute, UCL.
